About

We are a talent management firm that focuses on helping African talent navigate and understand the complex and ever changing entertainment business while creating new opportunities and maximize existing opportunities. We put structure around talent to help them mahout their career, build their brands, structure them as a business and help them generate multiple revenue streams.

We are firm believers in the idea that in order to best leverage our relationships and networks for the benefit of our talent there needs to be shared values between the two entities, both entities need to enhance each other and highlight each other’s strengths.

Philosophy

We are never just a collection of strategists and business people; we are a company of futurists, cultural influencers, entrepreneurs, fans and more. Our team is a diverse mix of creative entrepreneurs from different perspectives, backgrounds, and beliefs. We’re passionate about African talent, seldom satisfied and always asking 'what’s next'. Every member of our team is actively engaged in the creation of contemporary culture.

Services

Our services range from music singles and albums to digital content to merchandising and fashion. Our model leverages our artists popularity and helps us build additional income streams by monetizing their impact on youth culture and lifestyle.
